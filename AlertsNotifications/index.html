    Alerts and Notifications¶

    Overview¶

    CloudPi alerts notify teams about policy findings, budget thresholds, and workflow events. Notifications can be routed to email or ticketing systems.

    Alert Types¶

    • Policy alerts based on rule triggers
    • Budget alerts for threshold breaches
    • Workflow alerts for task or ticket status changes

    Notification Channels¶

    • Email via SMTP
    • Tickets via Jira, ServiceNow, or Azure DevOps

    Configuration¶

    1. Navigate to Workspace Settings.
    2. Enable alert types and choose notification channels.
    3. Test alerts with a pilot rule or threshold.

    Best Practices¶

    • Use dedicated distribution lists for email alerts.
    • Route high-severity findings to ticketing systems.
    • Review alert rules quarterly to reduce noise.
