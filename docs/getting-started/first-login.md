# First Login

## Accessing CloudPi

### Step 1: Navigate to Login Page
Open your web browser and go to the CloudPi URL provided by your administrator.

### Step 2: Choose Authentication Method

#### Username/Password Login
1. Enter your **email address**
2. Enter your **password**
3. Click **Sign In**

#### SSO Login (If Configured)
1. Click **Sign in with Azure AD** (or your configured SSO provider)
2. You'll be redirected to your organization's login page
3. Enter your corporate credentials
4. Approve consent (first time only)
5. You'll be redirected back to CloudPi

## Initial Configuration Tasks

After logging in for the first time, administrators should:

1. **Cloud Onboarding** - Connect AWS/Azure/GCP accounts
2. **Cost Assignment** - Set up rules and projects
3. **User Management** - Invite team members
4. **Budget Setup** - Define budget periods and amounts
5. **Tag Policies** - Configure mandatory and optional tags

Regular users should:

1. **Explore Dashboards** - Familiarize yourself with available reports
2. **Review Projects** - Understand your assigned projects
3. **Set Preferences** - Configure notifications and alerts
4. **Check Permissions** - Understand what you can access
